Getting Here

Found on Partizanska, Feelmotion 3.0 will be hosted in FIOFA. Easy to find, you just travel down the Boulevard of Macedonian Educators towards the lake (if you hit the water, you’ve gone TOO far!). Keep an eye open for Partizanska Street and when you see it turn left! Continue, until you see FIOFA on your left.
If this is too confusing just click on this and Google will guide you!

Bernd Buder

With many many years of experience in film festivals and organizations, Bernd has worked as director for Berliner Filmkunsthaus Babylon, while scouting out talent at other festivals, particularly in the Balkans. He now joins us as the consultant for Former Yugoslavian Nations at Berlinale – not a bad position to hold – and programmes for Cinedays for Balkan film and the wider European cinema. You can also find him writing various articles as a critic in the German periodical Filmdienst.

We are absolutely thrilled that these two legends have spared time in their busy schedules to join us for Feelmotion 3.0!

×

Ivo Mathé

Ivo Mathé made history by becoming the first CEO of Czech Television in 1992. He served Vice President of the European Broadcasting Union during that time (no mean feat!) and in 1999, he became a member of the Board of Trustees for CTU and AMU – while working as Head of the Office of the Czech President. In his spare time, he managed to teach at FAMU where he had previously studied.

We are absolutely thrilled that these two legends have spared time in their busy schedules to join us for Feelmotion 3.0!
