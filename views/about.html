Transforming the way people see the world, through film.

FEELMOTION is designed by and aimed at the next generation of filmmakers from the Balkans and beyond. We are deeply committed to showcasing the best student made films and the up-and-coming talents of the future.

Created as a way of bringing together like-minded film lovers and maintaining relationships from different parts of the world, Feelmotion is not a typical film festival – there is no serious competition between filmmakers here, just a wish for fellow enthusiasts to build a better filmmaking future – where we can meet as contemporary filmmakers.

It is a rare opportunity for filmmakers to meet together and even rarer still to meet in a place as beautiful and photogenic as Ohrid.

Through our programme of screenings, workshops and activities, we hope to make both professional and personal friendships at Feelmotion and garner a much-needed international recognition for filmmaking in the Balkans.

We seek to find real emotion at Feelmotion!
